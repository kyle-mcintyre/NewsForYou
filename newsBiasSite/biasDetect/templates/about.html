About the Project

Usage

Paste any politically charged article into the textbox on the "Home" page, then hit submit.
The AI machine will evaluate the article and return either a score of "Liberal Bias", "Conservative Bias" or "No Bias" detected.

Data

The data consists of ~2100 articles with about 700 articles from far left, far right, and neutral news sites.
News sites were determined from https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/.

AI Model

Each article was first summarized and then cleansed and vectorized in the following steps:
All lowercase -> remove all non-english characters -> remove all punctuation -> remove all stop words -> stem the text (Porter Stemming) -> tokenize the text -> TF IDF vectorize
I then created a Support-Vector-Machine from the vectorized articles.
